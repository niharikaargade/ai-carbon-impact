    FAQ: AI impact and carbon footprint

    What is AI carbon footprint?

    AI carbon footprint refers to emissions caused by electricity consumption in AI workloads, including inference in data centers.

    Can AI increase global warming risk?

    Yes, especially at scale. Large and frequent AI usage can raise data center electricity demand, and that can increase emissions where grids are still carbon intensive.

    Why does this calculator matter?

    It helps translate abstract AI energy use into understandable real-world equivalents so users can make lower-impact technology choices.
